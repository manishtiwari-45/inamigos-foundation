Who We Are

Get to Know Us Better

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). We are a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability, and tax-exempt benefits for donors. Our foundation is CSR-1 registered, allowing corporate partnerships, and NITI Aayog registered, aligning our work with national development goals.

We believe in the power of collective action. Every contribution drives food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability and social inclusion — across India.
